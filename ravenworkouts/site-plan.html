Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show beginners different types of workouts at the gym and how to use the equipment safely and effectively. It aims to help those beginners to feel more confident when starting out at the gym, which many people struggle with.

Audience

People who are new to working out at the gym and want a simple guide to using equipment and choosing workout types.

Dynamic elements

When users select a workout(such as bench press, squat, bicep curls) the site dynamically displays the related equipment and excercises/tips.
-Show/hide instructions for each excercise with a button or click event.
-Conditional messages display safety tips when workout is selected.
-use arrays to hold the excercise objects(name, description, image, tips, etc.)
-event listeners to respond to clicks on muscle group/equipment buttons.
-possible filter function for different muscle groups

Title: Learn the Techniques Paragraph: One of the biggest parts of getting results in the gym is knowing how to perform exercises the right way. On this site, youll find clear, beginner-friendly guides to a variety of basic workouts. Whether you know what muscle group you want to train or just need new ideas, you can browse through images and descriptions of different exercises, learn the proper technique, and see what muscles each one targets. Its a simple, easy-to-follow reference you can use before or during your workout.
